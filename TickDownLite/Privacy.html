Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2, 2025

Thank you for using TickDown Lite! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. We prioritize your privacy while providing a free, ad-supported service.

Summary: Your tasks, events, and personal content are stored locally on your device. We do not see them. We use third-party services (Google AdMob) to display advertisements, which may collect anonymous identifiers to show relevant ads.

1. Data Storage: Local First

TickDown Lite operates primarily offline regarding your content:

  • Your Content: Events, tasks, notes, and categories are stored in a local database on your device. We do not transmit this content to our servers.
  • Backups: Backup files are generated locally. You are responsible for storing them securely.

2. Advertising and Third-Party Data

To keep this app free, we display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

  • Ad Personalization: AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID (GAID) and other device signals (e.g., approximate location, app usage data) to serve personalized ads that are relevant to you.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising in your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Delete advertising ID).
  • GDPR/UK Consent: If you are located in the EEA or UK, we ask for your explicit consent before showing personalized ads. You can manage your choices at any time within the app settings.

For more information, please review Google's Advertising Privacy Policy.

3. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to collect anonymous usage data to improve app stability.

  • Collected Data: Crash reports, app launch time, and aggregate feature usage (e.g., "User created a task").
  • NOT Collected: We never track the text content of your tasks or events.

4. Permissions

We request specific permissions to provide core features:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To deliver your scheduled reminders.
  • USE_EXACT_ALARM: To ensure reminders ring at the exact time you set.
  • READ_CALENDAR: (Optional) To display your device's calendar events within the app. This data is read only for display and is not stored or transmitted by us.
  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to load advertisements and analytics.

5. Children's Privacy

TickDown Lite is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our ad settings are configured to comply with Google Play's Families Policy where applicable.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
