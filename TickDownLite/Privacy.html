Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 3, 2025

Summary: Your personal content (tasks, events) is stored locally on your device and is not seen by us. To keep the app free, we use third-party services for ads and handle optional in-app purchases through the Google Play Store.

1. Data Storage: Your Content is Yours

TickDown Lite follows a "Local First" architecture:

  • User Content: All tasks, events, notes, and categories you create are stored only in a local database on your device. We do not transmit this content to our servers.
  • Backups: Backup files are generated locally. You are solely responsible for storing and securing these files.

2. Monetization: Ads and In-App Purchases

To support development, TickDown Lite is monetized through advertisements and an optional one-time In-App Purchase (IAP).

Advertising

We display advertisements provided by Google AdMob and its mediation partners. These services help keep the app free.

  • Data for Ads: Our ad partners may use your device's Advertising ID (GAID) and other anonymous signals (like approximate location derived from your IP address) to serve personalized ads.
  • Opt-Out: You can manage personalized advertising in your Android device settings.
  • GDPR/UK Consent: For users in applicable regions, we request consent before showing personalized ads via Google's User Messaging Platform.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of our ad partners:

  • Google AdMob
  • IronSource
  • Unity Ads

In-App Purchase

TickDown Lite offers a one-time In-App Purchase ("Premium Unlock") to remove ads and unlock all features. All transactions are processed securely through the Google Play Store. We do not collect or store your payment information (e.g., credit card numbers). Your purchase history is managed by Google and is used by our app to verify and restore your premium status if you reinstall or change devices.

3. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics (by Google) to collect anonymous, aggregated usage data to help us identify crashes, fix bugs, and understand which features are most used. We never track the text content of your tasks or events.

4. Permissions We Request

We only request permissions essential for the app's functionality:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To deliver your scheduled reminders and alerts.
  • USE_EXACT_ALARM: To ensure your reminders fire at the precise time you set.
  • READ_CALENDAR: (Optional) To display your device's calendar events within the app if you enable this feature. This data is read only for display and is not stored or transmitted by us.
  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to load advertisements, analytics data, and verify purchases.
  • AD_ID: Required by our advertising partners to serve relevant ads and for analytics.

5. Children's Privacy

TickDown Lite is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our ad settings are configured to comply with Google Play's Families Policy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new terms.
