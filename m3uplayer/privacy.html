Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2026

M3U IPTV Player ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles user information.

1. No Content Provided

M3U IPTV Player is a media player only. The app does not provide, host, store, or distribute any media content. All playlists, streams, and credentials are added and managed entirely by the user.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

  • Playlist URLs and credentials are stored locally on your device only.
  • Usernames and passwords are stored in encrypted form using Android security mechanisms.
  • No playlist or stream data is transmitted to our servers.

3. Credentials & Security

Any login credentials entered by the user (such as Xtream Codes username/password) are encrypted and stored locally on the device. They are decrypted temporarily in memory only for playback purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdMob (for advertisements)
  • Google Play Services

These services may collect limited device-related information as per their own privacy policies. We do not control or access this data.

5. Advertisements

Advertisements, if enabled, are displayed only within the app interface and never injected into media streams. You may choose an ad-free option if available.

6. Children’s Privacy

This application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.
