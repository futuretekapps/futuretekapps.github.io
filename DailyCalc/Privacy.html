Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 16, 2026

DailyCalc ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects information when you use DailyCalc.

Information We Collect

DailyCalc itself does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, third-party services used in the app may collect limited technical and usage information automatically.

Data You Enter Into Calculators

DailyCalc includes health and fitness calculators (such as BMI, BMR, and TDEE) and financial calculators (such as loan/EMI, investment, SIP, and SWP). Any data you enter into these calculators—including your height, weight, age, gender, activity levels, loan amounts, interest rates, and investment figures—is processed entirely locally on your device to perform the requested calculations.

We do not collect, transmit to external servers, or share this health-related or financial data with any third parties, including our advertising or analytics partners.

Reports and PDF Export

Some calculators let you generate a PDF report (for example, a loan or investment summary). These reports are created locally on your device from the values you entered and are stored temporarily in the app's private cache. A report only leaves your device if you choose to share it using your device's share options. We do not upload or store your reports on any server.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Advertising networks served through Google AdMob mediation, which may include Unity Ads, ironSource (Unity LevelPlay), and InMobi
  • Google User Messaging Platform (Consent management for ads)
  • Firebase Analytics (Usage Analytics)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)
  • Google Play Billing (In-app purchases)

These services may collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • App interactions (excluding your health and financial calculator inputs)
  • Crash logs
  • Advertising identifiers
  • General location (such as country/region, used to serve appropriate ads)
  • Performance diagnostics

Each third-party advertising network processes data in accordance with its own privacy policy. You can review them here: Google / AdMob, Unity Ads / ironSource, and InMobi.

Advertising and Consent

DailyCalc shows advertisements provided by Google AdMob and its mediation partners. Where required by law (for example, for users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland), we use the Google User Messaging Platform to request your consent before serving personalized ads. You can change your choices at any time through your device's ad settings.

In-App Purchases

DailyCalc offers an optional purchase to remove ads and unlock premium features. Payments are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment card details; your purchase status is stored locally on your device.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix crashes and bugs
  • Enhance user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements

Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention: DailyCalc stores your calculation history, favorites, and application preferences locally on your device. We retain this local data only for as long as the app remains installed on your device. We do not transmit, store, or retain this personal data on any external servers.

Data collected by third-party services (such as Google AdMob, its mediation partners, or Crashlytics) is retained in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Data Deletion: Because your personal data is stored exclusively on your local device and not on our servers, you have full control over its deletion. You can delete all of your user data at any time by taking either of the following actions:

  • Uninstalling the app: Uninstalling DailyCalc from your device will automatically and permanently delete all locally stored data associated with the app.
  • Clearing App Data: You can clear the app's data by navigating to your device's Settings > Apps > DailyCalc > Storage > Clear Data.

You can also clear your calculator history from within the app.

If you have questions about data deletion or wish to make a request regarding data collected by third-party analytics, please contact us at futuretekapps@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

DailyCalc is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and ensure the security of the app.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Clear app data from your device settings (this will delete your calculation history)
  • Clear your calculation history from within the app
  • Reset advertising identifiers from your device settings
  • Disable personalized ads in your Google settings
