Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

DailyCalc ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects information when you use DailyCalc.

Information We Collect

DailyCalc itself does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, third-party services used in the app may collect limited technical and usage information automatically.

Health and Fitness Data

DailyCalc includes health and fitness calculators (such as BMI, BMR, and TDEE). Any data you enter into these calculators—including your height, weight, age, gender, and activity levels—is processed entirely locally on your device to perform the requested calculations.

We do not collect, transmit to external servers, or share this health-related data with any third parties, including our advertising or analytics partners.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Firebase Analytics (Usage Analytics)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)

These services may collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • App interactions (excluding health data inputs)
  • Crash logs
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Performance diagnostics

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix crashes and bugs
  • Enhance user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements

Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention: DailyCalc stores your calculation history, favorites, and application preferences locally on your device. We retain this local data only for as long as the app remains installed on your device. We do not transmit, store, or retain this personal data on any external servers.

Data collected by third-party services (such as Google AdMob or Crashlytics) is retained in accordance with their respective privacy policies.

Data Deletion: Because your personal data is stored exclusively on your local device and not on our servers, you have full control over its deletion. You can delete all of your user data at any time by taking either of the following actions:

  • Uninstalling the app: Uninstalling DailyCalc from your device will automatically and permanently delete all locally stored data associated with the app.
  • Clearing App Data: You can clear the app's data by navigating to your device's Settings > Apps > DailyCalc > Storage > Clear Data.

If you have questions about data deletion or wish to make a request regarding data collected by third-party analytics, please contact us at futuretekapps@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

DailyCalc is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and ensure the security of the app.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Clear app data from your device settings (this will delete your calculation history)
  • Reset advertising identifiers from your device settings
  • Disable personalized ads in your Google settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
