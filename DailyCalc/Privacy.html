Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 6, 2026

DailyCalc ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects information when you use DailyCalc.

Information We Collect

DailyCalc itself does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

However, third-party services used in the app may collect limited technical and usage information automatically.

Health and Fitness Data

DailyCalc includes health and fitness calculators (such as BMI, BMR, and TDEE). Any data you enter into these calculators—including your height, weight, age, gender, and activity levels—is processed entirely locally on your device to perform the requested calculations.

We do not collect, transmit to external servers, or share this health-related data with any third parties, including our advertising or analytics partners.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Firebase Analytics (Usage Analytics)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)

These services may collect information such as:

  • Device information
  • App interactions (excluding health data inputs)
  • Crash logs
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Performance diagnostics

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix crashes and bugs
  • Enhance user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements

Local Data Storage

DailyCalc stores calculation history locally on your device using a local database. This information never leaves your device unless explicitly shared or exported by you.

Children's Privacy

DailyCalc is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information and ensure the security of the app.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Clear app data from your device settings
  • Reset advertising identifiers from your device settings
  • Disable personalized ads in your Google settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
